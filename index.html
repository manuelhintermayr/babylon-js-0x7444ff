babylon-js-0x7444ff

A Babylon.js port of SahilK-027's 0x7444ff — 19 interactive WebGL2 demos rebuilt in Babylon.js.

Click any tile to launch the demo. Each demo's underlay credits the original creator @SahilK-027 and links back to the source repo.

Source: github.com/manuelhintermayr/babylon-js-0x7444ff

Kaleidoscope

Vanilla WebGL fragment kaleidoscope

Electric Waves

100 Catmull-Rom tubes + traveling particles

Black Pearl Flag

Wave-displaced flag with pearl logo

Abstract Pattern

Stefan Gustavson 2D Perlin plane

Peacock

256² particle peacock with mouse-painted glow

Particles-GPGPU

GPGPU flow-field particles around four GLB models

Iron Man Hologram

GLB mesh with glitch + fresnel-rim hologram shader

Space Distortion

RTT-based mask-warp under the cursor

Poly Ele

Polygonal elephant with per-tri pixelisation + bloom

Glowing Phoenix

GLB phoenix with mosaic + ember + 3-pass post-processing

Advanced Abstract Pattern

1M-vertex Perlin terrain + bokeh + parallax

Transformers Transition

Texture blend transition through square / hexagon / circle SDFs

Wobbly Sphere

PBR icosahedron with simplex-noise displacement

Organic Pattern

3D Perlin noise on a perspective plane

2D Day-Night SDF

Procedural day-night cycle with mountains, sun, stars

Scrolling Textures

FBM-displaced water texture loop

Grass

64 000 wind-swayed grass blades + grid floor

Voronoi Electric Pattern

Caustic-Voronoi cube with ~80 GUI uniforms

Spectral Flow

Spectral caustic-Voronoi plane with cosine palette
